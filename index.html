What therapy offers

Understanding yourself better

What brings people to therapy?

Seeking therapy can be an opportunity to make sense of your past, reflect on the present, and prepare for the future. We can all experience difficulties: sometimes we feel stuck with an issue, or a stressful situation leaves us feeling unable to cope. Being listened to with genuine empathy can help you work through what's causing distress.

What therapy can help you do

  • Identify your problems clearly
  • Clarify your thoughts and feelings
  • Find a more satisfying way of living
  • Make decisions that lead to change
  • Develop a deeper understanding of yourself and your relationships
  • Enhance your relationships with others

About Amanda

My approach

My approach to counselling and psychotherapy is person-centred and humanistic — I work in a non-directive and non-judgemental way, taking my cues from you. Sessions are a confidential, reflective and caring space to explore what's on your mind.

I have substantial experience of working within statutory, voluntary and private sector counselling services.

You can also find me on the Counselling Directory.
